Real-time Notifications Monitoring

Track and display notifications or alerts as they occur instantly or near-instantly. This feature enables users or administrators to observe and receive immediate updates regarding specific events, activities, or changes within the system.

Effectively manage and monitor your kids' screen time with our parental control application. Set healthy boundaries, schedule device usage, and gain insights into their digital habits to promote balanced and responsible screen time management.
